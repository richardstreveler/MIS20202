Learning Objectives

After completing this assignment you will be able to use various software applications to collaborate with others by:

  • agreeing on a division of work items
  • using web conferencing software to share files
  • using web conferencing software to share desktops

The exercises in this assignment must be done in the order presented.

Assignment Overview

This assignment requires working with others in this course.

You are strongly advised to find seek out and meet with your group members the week before this assignment. Review these instructions and plan on how you will complete this assignment.

The purpose of this assignment is to learn how businesses and other entities can conduct business at a distance using web conferencing and other internet based tools.

The internet has provided tools to conduct business in ways not possible 10 years ago. Competitive advantage to a certain extent is based on how fast a business can execute a plan or strategy.
The power of web based collaboration should not be overlooked.
Becoming adept at collaborating with remotely located colleagues and clients will be expected by most businesses.Some items to consider are:

  • Are we using the most effective tool set?
  • Does my business need to update it's technology?
  • What business opportunities may be lost if we do not use web collaboration?
  • What can collaboration technology and software do for our business?

This assignment will explore ways to use web collaboration to simplify and expedite working at a distance. You are encouraged to explore on your own to find additional features that will make your life and present or future jobs easier.

Procedural Matters

  1. The student doing step 1 (student 1)creates and shares the initial Google Sheets file with all the group members. If there are others in the meeting room, you can use Zoom to share your screen so that others can see what you are doing.
  2. The group chooses another student (student 2) to make screen shots as the work on the Google Sheet and the Geo Chart progresses-at least one screen shot of each.
  3. Student 2 sends the screen shots to student 3, who puts these into a PowerPoint presentation documenting your work.
  4. Feel free to use One Drive for Business, Google Drive or Zoom to work on the PowerPoint presentation together.
  5. Make use of the web conferencing application tools to share files and chat/communicate.
    Note that in Zoom, file sharing is accomplished using the Chat window.
  6. The PowerPoint presentation will consist of:
    1. Title slide listing all group members, the group number
    2. Slide containing a screen shot of the Google Sheet with data
    3. Slide containing a screen shot of the Google Chart with data
    4. Slide containing a screen shot of Zoom Meeting Window with Chat and Participant Windows
    5. Other slides you feel appropriate- the Zoom meeting, the use of Skype, IM, email or any other tool(s) used to complete this project, but that is not required.
